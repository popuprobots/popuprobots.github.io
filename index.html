Who Comes to PopUp Robots?

PopUp Robots is an informal meeting of people who are interested in Robots, Automation, Arduino, Nodebots, and IoT devices.

We welcome all skill levels and background. If you are a web developer and you already know some JavaScript then you can dive right in with Nodebots. If you are new to robotics, you can come out and learn. If you have been tinkering with Arduinos, you can extend that knowledge and do more.

When & Where is it?

We meet at the 757 Makerspace every third Thursday of the month in their awesome space. There is a brief presentation on a different concept and then an open build phase where you are encouraged to show off your latest project ask questions, and work on your own projects and get help from the other members. We can help you find the resources you need to make your project bigger and better. We are proud of our community and can't wait to meet you.
